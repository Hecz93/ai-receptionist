AI Agent Receptionist FAQ

What is an AI agent receptionist?

An AI agent receptionist is an advanced virtual assistant that uses natural language processing to answer phone calls, interact with customers, qualify leads, and book appointments 24/7 without human intervention.

How does it handle lead qualification?

The AI receptionist asks specific screening questions tailored to your business. It identifies high-intent callers and automatically pushes their data to your CRM or schedules them on your calendar.

Can it sync with my existing calendar?

Yes, Nexus AI integrates seamlessly with Google Calendar, Outlook, Calendly, and most major CRM systems to ensure your schedule is always up to date.
